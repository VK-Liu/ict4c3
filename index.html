      The 1st International Workshop ICT for Combating Climate Crisis(ICT4C3)

          in conjunction with The 32nd

          International Telecommunication Networks and

    Application Conference (ITNAC 2022 https://itnac.org.au)  

    Nov 30-Dec 2, 2022 in Wellington, New Zealand 

     Hybrid Workshop - online and face-to-face  

​

   

   The accepted ICT4C3 Workshop papers will be with the ITNAC Conference contents submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore, Google Scholar and other Abstracting and Indexing (A&I) databases. The Workshop is technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology (SIGHT), IEEE Vehicular Technology Society (VTS) and IEEE Communications Society (ComSoc) Technical Committee on Big Data (TCBD). We welcome the support of the IEEE and encourage participants to join the IEEE and IEEE Society Technical Committees. 

Authors of the Selected Papers will be invited to submit their extended versions to the related Special Issues of The Future Internet Journal (https://www.mdpi.com/journal/futureinternet)  which is published by Multidisciplinary Digital Publishing Institute (MDPI), and The Green Technologies and Sustainability Journal  https://www.keaipublishing.com/en/journals/green-technologies-and-sustainability/  which is co-published by Elsevier and China Science Publishing & Media Ltd., jointly called KeAi.
